Description:

Laserlike was a provider of an online content search engine platform designed to deliver high quality information and diverse perspectives on any topic from around the entire web. The company's platform used machine learning to source information on topics users care about with news, web, video, local and more, enabling users to get timely and relevant updates of their interests.

Apple bought the company in 2019. As part of the acquisition, the Laserlike app was removed from the App Store and their team and technology were incorporated into Apple to help them strengthen its artificial intelligence efforts, including Siri.
