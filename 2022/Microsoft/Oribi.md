Description:

Oribi is the developer of a marketing analytics tool designed to help businesses become data-driven. 

The company's tool utilizes artificial intelligence to offer advertisement insights tools to provide marketing teams with statistics and actionable insights that are not available on social media platforms, enabling clients to create informed online campaigns and understand their marketing channels, activities, and web pages in order to reach impactful results.

Microsoft acquired the company in 2022. As part of the acquisition, Oribi became part of LinkedIn. Through the integration of Oribi's technology into their marketing solutions platform, their customers would benefit from enhanced campaign attribution to optimize the ROI of their advertising strategies.

Acquired on February 28, 2022
