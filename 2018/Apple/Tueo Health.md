Tueo Health was a developer of asthma monitoring system designed to improve asthma control in children. The company's application integrated monitoring system uses a contactless sensor for monitoring and controlling asthma, enabling patients and their families to measure treatment progress and outcomes.

Apple first acquired part of the company's core technology in 2018 and later the whole company. This acquisition was made to continue to roll more health-oriented features in its hardware products.
