Description:

Akonia Holographics was a developer of advanced optical technologies designed to achieve revolutionary advances in holographic data storage. The company's technologies employed a proprietary volume holographic media and know-how to uniquely enable thin, transparent smart glass lenses that display vibrant, full-color, wide field-of-view images.

Apple acquired the comoany in 2018. As part of the acquisition, the Akonia' team joined Apple and the company was shut down.
